Our Beginning

The Story Behind Our Mission

Aura was founded in 2015 by a small group of passionate spiritual practitioners who believed that authentic tarot reading and spiritual guidance should be accessible to everyone, everywhere.

What began as intimate readings in a small crystal shop has blossomed into a global platform connecting thousands of seekers with gifted spiritual guides. Our journey is rooted in the belief that every soul deserves clarity, compassion, and cosmic wisdom.

Today, we serve clients across 40+ countries, offering live sessions that bridge the ancient wisdom of tarot with the convenience of modern technology.

Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To provide authentic, compassionate, and accessible spiritual guidance that empowers individuals to navigate life's challenges, discover their inner wisdom, and align with their highest purpose. We are committed to maintaining the integrity of ancient spiritual practices while embracing modern technology to reach seekers worldwide.

Our Vision

To become the world's most trusted spiritual guidance platform, creating a global community where seekers and gifted practitioners come together in a space of love, light, and transformation. We envision a world where spiritual wellness is recognized as an essential part of holistic well-being.
